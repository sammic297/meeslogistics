  • How do I know what size category my item fit?

    We try to define sizes in the simplest terms that everyone understands, mostly relative to the vehicle required. The other alternative would be to use a scale to determine the weight especially for nationwide and international shipping.

    SMALL ITEMS: Food packs, envelopes, small parcels & wraps.

    MEDIUM ITEMS: Shoe boxes, bags, containers (5ltr size).

    LARGE ITEMS: Microwaves, Fridge, Large bags e.t.c.

  • Do you collect Payment on Delivery (POD)?

    Yes, we do. POD in this context refers strictly to our drivers collecting money on your behalf at dropoff and NOT delivery fees at pickup. We pay out next day. All records of cash colected are available on your dashboard.
